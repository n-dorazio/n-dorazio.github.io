Orbit

Xcode • Swift • SwiftUI

Orbit is an app currently in development with the goal of empowering every university student with the ability to find and approach other people who share similar interests. At its core, Orbit addresses the isolation people feel despite living in a highly connected world. Its goal is to create a community where users—referred to as "astronauts" —can explore social connections, make friends, and feel a sense of belonging. It targets individuals seeking to meet new people and build genuine relationships in a safe, personalized, and engaging environment.
