    • Orbit

    iOS Social Networking App

    iOS Development

    Orbit is an iOS app built from the ground up with a team of students, entirely using Swift and Appwrite. We are on a mission to empower every university student the ability to find and approach other people who share similar interests, ultimately helping each one of us create lifelong friendships.

    At its core, Orbit addresses the isolation people feel despite living in a highly connected world. Its goal is to create a community where users—referred to as "astronauts"—can explore social connections, make friends, and feel a sense of belonging.

    View Source Code

    Project Goals

    Combat Social Isolation

    Address the paradox of feeling alone despite living in a highly connected world by creating genuine social connections.

    Student Community Building

    Empower university students to find and connect with peers who share similar interests and values.

    Lifelong Friendships

    Create a platform that facilitates the formation of meaningful, lasting relationships among students.

    Development Process

    Key Features

    Interest Matching

    Filtering and sorting options to match students based on shared interests, hobbies, and academic pursuits.

    Safe Environment

    Built-in safety features and verification systems to ensure a secure networking experience.

    Real-time Messaging

    Instant messaging system for seamless communication between matched students.

    Profile Customization

    Detailed user profiles with interests, photos, and personal information to facilitate better connections.
